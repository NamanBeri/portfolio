# Suggested Images for Your Portfolio

To make your website truly stand out, please gather the following images. You can download them from the internet (sites like Unsplash, Pexels, or Pinterest) or use your own photos.

## 1. The Scientist (Main Page)
*   **Hero Background:** A high-quality abstract image representing "Connectivity" or "Robotics".
    *   *Keywords:* Neural networks, constellations, circuit board art, futuristic blueprint.
*   **Profile Picture:** A professional photo of you (or a high-tech avatar if you prefer).
*   **Project Thumbnails:**
    *   **Robotics:** Image of a robot arm, rover, or ROS simulation.
    *   **IoT:** Circuit boards, sensors, or smart home setup.
    *   **AI/ML:** Data visualization, brain network graphic.
    *   **8-Bit Computer:** A close-up of your breadboard computer or a retro computing image.
    *   **Levitation:** An image of acoustic levitation or sound waves.

## 2. The Artist (Creative Page)
*   **Hero Background:** Something chaotic but beautiful.
    *   *Keywords:* Ink in water, galaxy nebula, abstract expressionism, stormy sky with light.
*   **Writing/Stories:** An image of a vintage typewriter, a quill, or an open book with glowing text.
*   **Music:** An artistic representation of sound waves, a violin/instrument, or musical notes floating.
